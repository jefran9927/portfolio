About Me

I am a highly motivated and adaptable Computer Engineering graduate from Politeknik Caltex Riau, holding an Associate of Applied Science in Informatics Engineering. Currently, I am expanding my skill set through a 6-month Data Science Bootcamp at Dibimbing.id. My professional experience includes a year at PT Pertamina Hulu Rokan, where I developed my skills in data management and visualization, transforming complex data sets into clear, actionable insights using tools like Power BI and MS Excel.

In addition to my role at PT Pertamina Hulu Rokan, I have held positions as a Programmer at the Biro Administrasi Pembangunan Sekretariat Daerah, where I developed a Monitoring and Evaluation System for regional budgets using Laravel, and as a Technical Assistant at PT Waskita Niagaprima, where I created a material inventory system website using PHP and managed maintenance databases.

I am proficient in multiple programming languages, including Python, PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (Next JS), and have intermediate to advanced expertise in frameworks such as Laravel and databases like MySQL. My ability to quickly learn and adapt to new environments, combined with hands-on experience and technical acumen, positions me to effectively contribute to dynamic and innovative teams. I am passionate about leveraging my skills to drive positive outcomes and make meaningful contributions to any organization I join.
